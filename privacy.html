Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2026

Our Privacy Mission

At PDF Universe, your privacy is our orbit. We understand that your documents contain sensitive information. Our core principle is simple: Your files belong to you, and we have no interest in keeping them.

The 2-Hour Rule: Every file you upload is automatically and permanently deleted from our servers within 2 hours of processing. We do not keep backups or archives of user content.

Data Handling & Security

We use industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption to protect your data during transit. Once your file arrives at our "station," it is processed by an automated system. No human being ever views your documents.

  • No Data Mining: We do not scan or sell your document content.
  • No File Storage: We are a utility, not a storage provider.

Information We Collect

To keep the Universe running smoothly, we collect minimal metadata:

  • Usage Stats: Anonymous data like browser type and tool usage to help us improve.
  • Cookies: Small files used to remember your preferences and analyze traffic via Google Analytics.

Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

We respect your right to access, delete, or limit the processing of your personal data. Even though we store almost no personal data, you can contact us at any time to request account deletion or to opt-out of cookies.
